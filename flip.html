Flip

Flip is a puzzle game about reflecting.

When you click on a line, the blue "R" will reflect across it. Try to get the blue "R" to the green one. Don't land on a red "R." Press R to restart.

You get 10 points for solving a level. You get 20 if you solve it without going over the par. You lose 5 points for hitting a red "R" or going out of the level, and you will restart the level.

Your game is saved automatically.
